A Scenic Walkway Along A River In Portugal

  • Type: Walkway
  • Location: 7.111 - 9.111
  • Country: Portugal
  • Condition: Good
  • Guard: Yes
  • Danger: Now

Winding their way through the scenic landscape along the Paiva river in Portugal, the ‘Paiva Walkways‘ were recently completed for explorations of the surroundings at close range. In a collaboration between the Arouca Municipality and engineer company Trimetrica, the 8km-long route was built to give visitors the chance to experience local natural beauty, including cliffs and waterfalls, as well as offering panoramic views of the valley. Additionally, the wooden pathway features a bridge crossing the river.
